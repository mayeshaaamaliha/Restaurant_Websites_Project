Privacy Policy

This Privacy Statement aims to assure our clients and other individuals that we are observing the appropriate level of personal data protection in compliance with the standards prescribed by Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the “Data Privacy Act”.

Personal Information to be Collected

  • Personal contact information which includes your name, address, email address, phone number, or any other information that would allow Golden Aurora to contact you;
  • Demographic information which includes your gender, date of birth, age, civil status, nationality, etc.; and
  • Non-personal information such as those provided by your device which may include your IP address, geolocation, operating system, browser type and version, and other machine identifiers, etc.

Collection Method

Your personal information may be collected, directly or indirectly, through any of the following means:

  • Forms, agreements and other similar or related documents you submitted;
  • Use and log-in to Golden Aurora’s website when you choose to fill out a ‘Contact Us’ form;
  • During availment of Golden Aurora’s services;
  • Communications and correspondences with our staff, whether in writing, verbally, or thru electronic means;
  • Footages from our CCTV cameras; and
  • Personal Information may also be obtained from third parties and other sources which have obtained your prior consent for disclosure or when Golden Aurora is legally allowed to acquire such information.
